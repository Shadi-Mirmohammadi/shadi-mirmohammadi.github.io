    2. Optically Tunable Terahertz Metasurface Absorber Based on Fe-Doped β-Ga₂O₃
      Seyedeh Shadi Mirmohammadi, and Berardi Sensale-Rodriguez

    3. Excitation-Selective Eu³⁺ Luminescence and Defect Signatures in GdScO₃ Single-Crystal Substrates
      Seyedeh Shadi Mirmohammadi, Afrouz Taherian, Yunshan Wang, Diego P. Fernandez, Michael A. Scarpulla, Steve Blair, and Berardi Sensale-Rodriguez
      Manuscript ready for submission to Optical Materials Express, 2026
      Tuning the UV excitation by  13 nm switches the emission of trace (1–2 ppm) Eu³⁺ in nominally undoped GdScO₃ substrates from blue-green to red (>10× ratio collapse), and a 1200 °C anneal erases the defect band — a fast, nondestructive optical screen for scandate substrates.
      Website
    4. Si QD Sensitization of Anthracene Triplet as Revealed by Time-Resolved EPR Spectroscopy
      Mandefro Y. Teferi, Sanoop Mambully Somasundaran, Shadi Mirmohammadi, Ming Lee Tang, Jens Niklas, and Oleg G. Poluektov
      Submitted to the Journal of the American Chemical Society, 2026
      First time-resolved EPR study of triplet energy transfer in Si QD–anthracene hybrids: the molecular triplet arrives fully spin-polarized (p(T₊₁) > p(T₀) > p(T₋₁)) from the QD’s dark exciton, and the linker chemistry (9EA vs. 9VA vs. BPEA) sets its delocalization.
      Website
