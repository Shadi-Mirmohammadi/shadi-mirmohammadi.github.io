    Shadi Mirmohammadi

    I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical & Computer Engineering at the University of Utah, working in the Sensale-Rodriguez and Blair labs. I design reconfigurable terahertz/mmWave photonic and RF devices with full-wave electromagnetic simulation (Ansys HFSS), and fabricate and characterize semiconductor thin films in the cleanroom — from ITO sputter deposition on Ga2O3 and sapphire to CW-THz spectroscopy. My work is aimed at silicon photonics, optical interconnects, and mmWave hardware.
