    Research Experience

    • 2023–Present

      Graduate Research Assistant

      Sensale-Rodriguez / Blair Labs, University of Utah
      • Designed and simulated optically tunable THz/mmWave metasurface modulators on Fe-doped β-Ga₂O₃ using full-wave 3D EM simulation (Ansys HFSS), achieving ~14 dB extinction ratio and ~3 dB insertion loss at ~300 GHz.
      • Performed S-parameter extraction and parametric optimization across 13 conductivity states, identifying critical-coupling conditions yielding ~82% absorption and ~99% reflection modulation depth (H-SRR design; IRMMW-THz 2026).
      • Developed ITO thin-film sputter deposition recipes (Denton Discovery 18) for transparent-electrode integration; validated films against reference sheet-resistance values by four-point probe and designed RTP anneal process (450°C, Ar).
      • Performed CW-THz transmission spectroscopy (Toptica photomixing spectrometer) on ITO/sapphire films to extract THz-frequency sheet resistance; reconciling THz-extracted vs. DC four-point-probe conductivity.
      • Built Python data-analysis pipelines (pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib) automating HFSS post-processing and measurement analysis; published open-source on GitHub.
      • Characterized excitation-selective Eu³⁺ emission switching and annealing-suppressible defect signatures in GdScO₃ single-crystal substrates via UV photoluminescence spectroscopy; first-author manuscript ready for submission to Optical Materials Express.
      • Analyzed UV photoresponsivity of GeO₂ photodetectors in a cross-group collaboration with Dr. Kai Fu's lab (Imteaz Rahaman), applying beam/detector-area corrections and calibrated power measurement.
    • 2023–2025

      Graduate Researcher

      Materials Chemistry, University of Utah
      • Synthesized and surface-engineered semiconductor quantum dots (CdSe, CdS, Si); developed electrochemical sol-gel thin-film processing and covalent surface-functionalization (hydrosilylation) routes; probed spin-correlated radical pairs via time-resolved EPR (Argonne National Lab).
      • Developed electrochemical sol-gelation of CdS quantum dots: cyclic voltammetry transforms colloidal QDs into interconnected 3D gel networks; correlated gelation kinetics/thermodynamics with charge transport and optical response, examining wurtzite vs. zinc-blende phase and size dependence.
      • Engineered CdSe–anthracene hybrid nanostructures for photon upconversion via triplet–triplet annihilation, achieving a record 16% efficiency — more than double prior reports.
      • Investigated triplet energy transfer (TET) across a library of QD–ligand conjugates, tuning QD size and ligand structure to optimize exciton diffusion and Dexter-type transfer efficiency (transient absorption, PL, EPR).
      • Designed, synthesized, and purified organic ligands (9VA, BPEA, NDI) using air-free Schlenk-line chemistry and chromatography; characterized by NMR and FTIR spectroscopy; optimized hydrosilylation initiators (AIBN, DTBP) and linker structures.
      • Probed spin-correlated radical-pair formation and the chiral-induced spin selectivity (CISS) effect using TR-EPR and chiroptical techniques, toward photocatalysis, asymmetric synthesis, and chiroptical photonics.
